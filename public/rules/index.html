Standards

Network Rules

All members of the Croydon Alliance Network are expected to uphold these standards.

⚠️ Violations of these rules may result in removal from the Croydon Alliance Network. These rules apply to all member servers and their leadership teams.
General Conduct
  • 1
    Treat all members of the network, including those from other alliance servers, with respect at all times. Harassment, discrimination, or targeted hostility of any kind will not be tolerated.
  • 2
    Do not engage in drama, public disputes, or negativity directed at CAN, its leadership, or other alliance servers. Issues should be raised privately through the appropriate channels.
  • 3
    No spreading of false information, rumours, or defamatory content regarding CAN, its members, or any alliance servers within the network.
  • 4
    Impersonating CAN staff or other server owners within the network is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate removal.
  • 5
    All communications within shared CAN spaces must remain appropriate. No NSFW content, excessive profanity, or content that would reflect poorly on the network.
  • 6
    Members are expected to report rule violations they witness rather than taking matters into their own hands. Vigilante action is not permitted.
  • 7
    Any activity that brings the Croydon Alliance Network into disrepute — whether inside or outside of CAN spaces — is grounds for review and potential removal.
Rules for Alliance Servers
  • 1
    Member servers must maintain active, responsive staff teams. A server with consistently absent leadership will be reviewed for removal from the network.
  • 2
    Server owners must remain reachable via the CAN leadership team within a reasonable timeframe. Prolonged non-communication (72+ hours without prior notice) is not acceptable.
  • 3
    Member servers must maintain the standards that were assessed during the application process. A significant drop in quality or activity will trigger a review.
  • 4
    Servers may not misrepresent their CAN membership status or use the CAN name or branding in a misleading way. Branding use must be approved by CAN leadership.
  • 5
    Alliance servers must not engage in hostile recruitment targeting other CAN member servers' staff or playerbase. Organic crossover is fine; targeted poaching is not.
  • 6
    Any significant changes to a server's ownership, structure, or direction must be communicated to CAN leadership in advance. Surprise changes may trigger a review of alliance status.
  • 7
    Member servers are expected to participate in network-wide events and communications where reasonable. Complete withdrawal from the network without explanation is grounds for removal.
  • 8
    Servers found to be operating in breach of Roblox or ER:LC community guidelines will be immediately suspended from CAN pending review. CAN is not responsible for the actions of member servers but reserves the right to act swiftly.
  • 9
    All servers must keep their Discord invite link shared with CAN up to date. An expired invite is not acceptable and should be updated immediately.

These rules are subject to update. CAN leadership reserves the right to make decisions in the best interest of the network, including in cases not explicitly covered here.
